# DORA Audit Findings and Report Template

A ready-to-use template for writing up single findings and for putting together the whole audit or gap-assessment report. Copy this file for each engagement and fill in the placeholders.

Version note: this template is method only. It does not carry any RTS or ITS numbers. Always record the RTS and ITS versions in force during your engagement in Part A and Part F.

## Part A: Report cover and executive summary

- Entity: [legal entity]
- Engagement type: [Internal audit / External assurance / Gap assessment]
- Scope: [pillars, functions, systems, third parties, period]
- Auditor or assessor: [name or firm]
- Report date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- Overall opinion or maturity: [for example, Partially compliant, overall maturity 2.6 out of 5]

Executive summary (half a page to one page). Sum up the objective, the scope, the overall conclusion, the number of findings by priority, and the biggest themes. State any limits on scope and which RTS and ITS versions you relied on.

## Part D: Detailed finding template (repeat for each finding)

Finding F-[NN]: [short title]

- Pillar and control ref: [for example, Pillar 2, INC-04]
- DORA reference: [for example, Art. 19, ITS on reporting]
- Priority: [P1 Critical / P2 High / P3 Medium / P4 Low]
- Current maturity: [0 to 5] Target: [0 to 5]

Condition (what we found). Plain description of what you saw, with the size of the problem. For example: "3 of 5 major incidents were not reported inside the first deadline."

Criteria (what DORA requires). The exact rule (article, RTS, or ITS) that the condition is measured against.

Cause (why it happened). The root cause. For example: unclear ownership, no runbook, or a tooling gap.

Consequence (so what). The regulatory, operational, financial, or reputational impact if it is not fixed.

Evidence. References to working papers, samples, documents, and logs you examined.

Recommendation. A specific, doable fix.

Management response. [Agreed / partly agreed / not agreed, with the reason.]

- Remediation owner: [name]
- Target date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- Re-assessment date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

## Part F: Scope limits and disclaimers

State anything left out of scope, evidence you could not get, any reliance on what management told you, and the exact RTS and ITS versions in force during the engagement. Note that this assessment is a point-in-time view and does not guarantee ongoing compliance.
