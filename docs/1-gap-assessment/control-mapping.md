# Control-to-Requirement Mapping

This matrix links each toolkit control to the relevant DORA articles and, where useful, the supplementing RTS or ITS. Use it to show coverage, to check evidence is complete, and to plan audit sampling.

Article numbers are indicative, based on Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 as published. The exact RTS and ITS instruments, numbers, and dates are maintained centrally in [regulatory-references.md](../regulatory-references.md) (the single source of truth); always confirm the current text on EUR-Lex and the ESA websites before you rely on any figure.
