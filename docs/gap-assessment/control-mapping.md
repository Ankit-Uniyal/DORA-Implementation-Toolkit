# Control-to-Requirement Mapping

This traceability matrix links each toolkit control to the relevant DORA articles (and, where applicable, the supplementing RTS/ITS). Use it to demonstrate coverage, evidence completeness, and to plan audit sampling.

> **Article numbers are indicative and based on Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 as published.** RTS/ITS references are described generically because their final numbering/dates should be confirmed on EUR-Lex and the ESA websites.

## How to use for coverage assurance

1. Confirm every applicable DORA article maps to at least one control (completeness).
2. For each control, confirm the primary evidence exists and is current (operating effectiveness).
3. Where an article is not applicable (e.g. TLPT for a non-designated entity), document the rationale rather than leaving it blank.
