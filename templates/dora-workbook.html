How this workbook works

On load it fetches the four source CSVs from the toolkit's main branch. If offline or blocked, it falls back to embedded headers so it still opens. Every blue cell is editable; the maturity tab drives the dashboard heat map in real time.

  • Maturity scoring: current score 0–5 vs target; gap and legal-fail computed live.
  • Heat map: per-pillar average, count below target, legal fails, and a RAG rating.
  • Overall readiness: mean of scored controls as a % of 5, minus a penalty for any legal fail.
  • Export: each tab re-serialises to CSV preserving the original column order.

Regulatory figures live in docs/regulatory-references.md (single source of truth). This tool does not give legal or investment advice.
