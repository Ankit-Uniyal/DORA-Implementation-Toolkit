## Status legend

- Approved and current: approved and within the review cycle.
- Draft: still being written.
- Overdue review: past its next-review date.
- Missing: required but does not exist (a gap).

## Maintenance checklist

- Every DORA-driven document has a named owner and approver.
- Board-level documents show management-body approval.
- Review dates are scheduled and tracked.
- Missing or overdue items are logged as gaps in the assessment.
