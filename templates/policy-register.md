## Status legend

- **Approved & current** — approved, within review cycle.
- **Draft** — under development.
- **Overdue review** — past next-review date.
- **Missing** — required but does not exist (gap).

## Maintenance checklist

- [ ] Every DORA-driven document has a named owner and approver.
- [ ] Board-level documents show management-body approval.
- [ ] Review dates are scheduled and tracked.
- [ ] Missing/overdue items are logged as gaps in the assessment.
