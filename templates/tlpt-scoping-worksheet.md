# Template: TLPT Scoping Worksheet

Supports DORA Art. 26 and 27 and the RTS on TLPT (lined up with TIBER-EU). Use this only if your competent authority has named your entity as in scope for threat-led penetration testing. Confirm with your authority before you start.

TLPT is run on live production systems and covers critical or important functions. It must follow the RTS and TIBER-EU method, with the authority validating the scope and a final attestation at the end.

## 4. Tester requirements checklist (Art. 27)

- Highest suitability and reputation.
- Technical and organisational capability; expertise in threat intel, penetration testing, and red teaming.
- Certification or use of a formal code of conduct or framework.
- (External testers) professional indemnity insurance in place.
- Conflicts of interest assessed and managed, especially for internal testers.
- Authority approval obtained where internal testers are used.

## 6. Deliverables and attestation

- Threat intelligence report.
- Red team test report (findings and evidence).
- Remediation plan with owners and dates.
- Summary provided to the authority.
- Authority attestation received, confirming the test met the requirements.

## 7. Risk management during testing

Write down the safeguards that protect production: agreed rules of engagement, "legs-up" procedures, real-time monitoring by the control team, escalation and abort criteria, and a check that clean-up was done after the test.
